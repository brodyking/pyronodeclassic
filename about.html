About Us

Pyronode.us is a website that hosts games that run off of HTML5/JS. All of our games are open source and we love working with the communities that are willing too!

Our Games

We make games. Duh. Most of them are lightweight 100mb games. One of our most prized games we have is called Klicker. Which is a online osu-like game that makes you hover over all the circles without messing up the order. It is to most people, a supprise to find how addicting and hard it really is!

Helping Us

All our games may be started by us, but we always get help from you guys! In klicker, we got help from multiple people! All of them getting put in the credits inside the game!
